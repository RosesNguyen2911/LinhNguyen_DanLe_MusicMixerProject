Farm Symphony - Drag and Drop Animal

Game Rules

  • Drag animals into empty wood platforms to place them.
  • Each animal can only be placed once on the stage.
  • Play: Play all animal sounds in order.
  • Stop: Stop all playing sounds immediately.
  • Replay: Replay the current arrangement of sounds.
  • Reset: Clear all animals from the stage.
